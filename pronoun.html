PRONOUNS (EXAM PERFECT ANSWER)

Definition

A pronoun is a word that is used in place of a noun.

Types of Pronouns

1. Personal Pronoun: Used for persons or things.
Examples: I, we, you, he, she, it, they

2. Possessive Pronoun: Shows ownership.
Examples: mine, yours, his, hers, theirs

3. Reflexive Pronoun: Refers back to the subject.
Examples: myself, yourself, himself, herself

4. Demonstrative Pronoun: Points out specific things.
Examples: this, that, these, those

5. Interrogative Pronoun: Used to ask questions.
Examples: who, what, which

6. Relative Pronoun: Connects clauses.
Examples: who, whom, whose, which, that

7. Indefinite Pronoun: Refers to non-specific persons or things.
Examples: someone, something, anyone, everything

Number

Singular: he, she, it

Plural: we, they

Functions

Subject: She is happy.

Object: I saw him.

Possession: This book is mine.
