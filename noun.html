NOUNS (EXAM PERFECT ANSWER)

Definition:

A noun is a word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.

Types of Nouns:

1. Proper Noun: India, Punjab, Ram

2. Common Noun: boy, city, dog

3. Abstract Noun: honesty, love, happiness

4. Concrete Noun: apple, chair, book

5. Collective Noun: team, class, family

6. Material Noun: gold, water, wood

Number:

Singular: book

Plural: books

Countable / Uncountable:

Countable: pen, apple

Uncountable: water, advice, furniture

Possessive:

Ram's book

Functions:

Subject: Ram runs

Object: She reads a book

Complement: She is a teacher
