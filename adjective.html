ADJECTIVES (EXAM PERFECT ANSWER)

Definition

An adjective is a word that describes or modifies a noun or pronoun.

Types of Adjectives

1. Descriptive Adjective: Describes quality.
Examples: beautiful, tall, happy

2. Quantitative Adjective: Shows quantity.
Examples: some, much, little

3. Demonstrative Adjective: Points out nouns.
Examples: this, that, these, those

4. Possessive Adjective: Shows ownership.
Examples: my, your, his, her

5. Interrogative Adjective: Used in questions.
Examples: which, what, whose

Degrees of Comparison

Positive: tall

Comparative: taller

Superlative: tallest

Functions

Describes noun: She has a beautiful dress.

Describes pronoun: He is very kind.
