VERBS (EXAM PERFECT ANSWER)

Definition

A verb is a word that shows action or state of being.

Types of Verbs

1. Action Verb: Shows action.
Examples: run, eat, play

2. Linking Verb: Links subject with complement.
Examples: is, am, are, was

3. Helping Verb: Helps main verb.
Examples: is, have, do, will

4. Transitive Verb: Has an object.
Example: She reads a book.

5. Intransitive Verb: No object.
Example: She sleeps.

Forms of Verb

V1: go

V2: went

V3: gone

Functions

Shows action: He runs.

Shows state: She is happy.
