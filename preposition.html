PREPOSITIONS (EXAM PERFECT ANSWER)

Definition

A preposition is a word placed before a noun or pronoun to show its relation with another word.

Common Prepositions

in, on, at, under, over, between, among

Types of Prepositions

1. Preposition of Time:
Examples: at, on, in

2. Preposition of Place:
Examples: in, on, under

3. Preposition of Direction:
Examples: to, into, onto

Examples

The book is on the table.

She is in the room.
