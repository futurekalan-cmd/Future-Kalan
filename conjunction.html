CONJUNCTIONS (EXAM PERFECT ANSWER)

Definition

A conjunction is a word that joins words, phrases, or sentences.

Types of Conjunctions

1. Coordinating Conjunction:
Examples: and, but, or, so

2. Subordinating Conjunction:
Examples: because, although, if, while

3. Correlative Conjunction:
Examples: either...or, neither...nor, both...and

Examples

She is tired but happy.

I will come if you call me.
