ADVERBS (EXAM PERFECT ANSWER)

Definition

An adverb is a word that modifies a verb, adjective, or another adverb.

Types of Adverbs

1. Adverb of Manner: How something happens.
Examples: quickly, slowly, happily

2. Adverb of Time: When something happens.
Examples: now, yesterday, soon

3. Adverb of Place: Where something happens.
Examples: here, there, everywhere

4. Adverb of Frequency: How often.
Examples: always, often, never

5. Adverb of Degree: How much or to what extent.
Examples: very, too, quite

Functions

Modifies verb: She runs quickly.

Modifies adjective: Very beautiful girl.

Modifies adverb: Runs very fast.
